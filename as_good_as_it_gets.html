this is as good as it gets

you're as good as it gets

- as it's ever gonna get

I hold on for dear life

yet slip into your depths

deeper and further

later you'll blame me for the hurt

and the scars I'd have left

a heart broken into and pride's theft

but tomight is as good as regret

tomorrow,

we'll deal with regret
